# Dark Patterns in Digital Pre-Purchase Environments

## Overview

Bachelor's thesis examining the impact of dark patterns in digital pre-purchase environments on Indian online consumers.

## Research Methodology

- 6 qualitative interviews
- 73 survey respondents
- Pearson Correlation Analysis
- Linear Regression
- ANOVA
- Logistic Regression

## Key Focus Areas

- Consumer behaviour
- Social media advertising
- Dark patterns
- Digital marketing ethics
- Purchase decision making

## Key Findings

The study explored how manipulative design techniques influence consumer decision-making and highlighted the importance of transparency and ethical digital marketing practices.
