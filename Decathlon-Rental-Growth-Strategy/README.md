# Decathlon – Rental Services Growth Strategy Project

## Overview

Developed a strategic growth plan for Decathlon's sports equipment rental business as part of an international consulting project. The project focused on identifying opportunities to expand market share, improve customer experience, strengthen sustainability initiatives, and enhance the long-term competitiveness of Decathlon's rental ecosystem. :contentReference[oaicite:0]{index=0}

## Objective

To evaluate Decathlon's existing rental offering and develop actionable recommendations that improve customer adoption, operational efficiency, and sustainability while supporting the company's circular economy objectives. :contentReference[oaicite:1]{index=1}

## Methodology

- Conducted industry and market analysis of the European sports equipment rental sector.
- Applied strategic frameworks including SWOT, VRIO, PESTEL, Porter’s Five Forces, Business Model Canvas, and ERRC.
- Evaluated customer segments, rental behaviour patterns, and sustainability trends.
- Assessed Decathlon's competitive positioning and growth opportunities within the rental market. :contentReference[oaicite:2]{index=2}

## Key Findings

- Growing consumer preference for access-over-ownership presents significant opportunities for rental-based business models.
- Decathlon's strong brand recognition, extensive product portfolio, and supply chain capabilities create a competitive advantage.
- Limited awareness of rental services and digital experience gaps restrict customer adoption.
- Sustainability-focused consumers increasingly favour rental and circular economy solutions.
- Significant opportunity exists to expand market share through improved digital integration and targeted customer segmentation. :contentReference[oaicite:3]{index=3}

## Strategic Recommendations

- Develop a dedicated digital rental platform to simplify booking and customer engagement.
- Design rental-specific products focused on durability, modularity, and ease of maintenance.
- Implement data-driven inventory management and predictive maintenance systems.
- Introduce subscription-based rental models for frequent users.
- Strengthen customer acquisition through targeted marketing and strategic partnerships.
- Expand circular economy initiatives through repair, refurbishment, and recycling programs. :contentReference[oaicite:4]{index=4}

## Outcome

Delivered a comprehensive strategic roadmap aimed at increasing rental adoption, improving operational efficiency, enhancing sustainability performance, and strengthening Decathlon's position within the growing sports equipment rental market. :contentReference[oaicite:5]{index=5}
