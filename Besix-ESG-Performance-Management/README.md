# BESIX ESG Performance Management Strategy

## Overview

Developed a sustainability-focused performance management framework for BESIX as part of an international consulting project. The project aimed to align employee performance, leadership development, and operational decision-making with Environmental, Social, and Governance (ESG) objectives.

## Project Scope

- Evaluated ESG challenges and opportunities within the construction industry
- Designed ESG-focused leadership and employee training programmes
- Developed environmental, social, and governance KPIs for performance evaluation
- Proposed a Resource Marketplace model to support circular economy practices
- Created implementation roadmaps, cost estimates, and governance recommendations

## Key Deliverables

### ESG Training Framework
- ESG Fundamentals for Senior Leaders
- Role-Specific ESG Skills Development Programme
- ESG-Focused Career Development Pathways

### Performance Management KPIs
- Environmental KPIs covering waste diversion, carbon emissions, renewable energy usage, and green certifications
- Social KPIs focused on employee wellbeing, ESG engagement, and workforce development
- Governance KPIs measuring transparency, stakeholder engagement, ESG leadership, and innovation

### Resource Marketplace Proposal
- Designed a circular economy platform leveraging BESIX's Material Passport system
- Developed implementation roadmap including system creation, pilot launch, and scaling strategy
- Identified regulatory requirements and compliance considerations for the French market

## Outcomes

- Created a comprehensive ESG performance management strategy aligned with long-term sustainability objectives
- Established measurable frameworks to track ESG progress across organisational functions
- Developed recommendations to strengthen stakeholder engagement, operational efficiency, and sustainability performance
